- **Anchoring** — lead with the raw AED figure before the percentage; the absolute number does the emotional work.
- **Social proof via ranking** — Slide 1 shows Palm beating every other Dubai luxury segment, making the claim feel consensual, not promotional.
- **Loss aversion** — TikTok caption reframes the viewer as the one who *sold early* (Sarwa's exact move). Pain > gain.
- **Concreteness** — "Signature Villa" + specific frond language in the first comment converts abstract upside into a literal, visualisable asset.
- **Open loop** — Slide 3's question ("How much higher can Palm go?") + Slide 4's gated report create DM intent rather than passive likes.

Global specs — apply to every slide unless overridden.
